The Samsung Chromebook 3 delivers everything you need from easy web browsing to built-in security features to a long battery life in a compact, durable design. Weighing only 2.54 pounds and measuring 0.7-inches thick, the Chromebook 3 is easy to take with you anywhere you go. The Chromebook 3 has a long battery life of 11 hours, so you can check email, watch videos, and work on documents throughout the day without having to recharge it. Thanks to the spill-resistant design, you can hand it to the kids or use it in the kitchen without any worry. Offering vivid picture quality and an ergonomic keyboard, the Samsung Chromebook 3 is a simple and affordable solution for staying connected online.
